📋 Arrival Checklist
  1. Thank you for showing up!
  2. ⌚ AFTER you arrive Record your Attendance using the QR code available ON SITE and sign out when you leave.
  3. You have been assigned a truck number. See below for your assignment. Stick to the assignment. If you don't see your name don't worry, email me or come find me Saturday. Apologies if this was missed.
  4. Gather, say hello, grab some donuts and coffee and stretch.
  5. We will announce when it's time to saddle up and gather in your Truck Zone and then you are off to the races.
  6. We want to make sure that the scout drivers stick with the truck and help shuttle scouts from one drop to another. We are in need of drivers to take MORE than just one scout. Please help and keep your group together.
